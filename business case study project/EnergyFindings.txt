💡 Insights for Smart City Energy Data:-

1. Which zones are energy hotspots or inefficient?

Ans. Central and North zones are the major energy hotspots 
with higher power consumption and outage levels.

2. What consumer type uses the most power?

Ans. Industrial consumers use the highest amount of electricity 
among all consumer types.

3. Are certain times/days correlated with high peak usage?

Ans. Peak energy usage is generally higher during weekdays due to 
commercial and industrial activities.

4. Are there areas with unreliable power supply (frequent outages)?

Ans. Some zones experience frequent outages,
indicating unreliable power supply and infrastructure issues.
